Fellini's Roma

Federico Fellini's homage to his adopted city, released in 1972

About

This film is a portrayal of Rome's history, from its ancient origins to more recent 20th century events, as perceived by Fellini. Past and present, acnient and modern, personal and universal are woven together throughout the film.

Synopsis

Roma is made up of a number of seemingly unrelated sequences, including Fellini's childhood, his move to Rome as a young man and wartime experience. These are mixed with present-day sequences that blend fantasy and reality, such as the Ecclesiastical fashion show (see below).
