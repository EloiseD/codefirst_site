Ladri di Biciclette

Bicycle Thieves

Vittorio De Sica's 1948 neorealist classic

About

This film was made in the chaotic post-war period in Italy on the eve of the 1948 general elections, a moment of great political change in Italy.

Synopsis

The film follows the unemployed father of a young family living on the outskirts of Rome in 1948. He is offered a job that requires him to find his stolen bicycle. The film tracks his desperate search for the machine.
