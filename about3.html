Cario Diario

Dear Diary

Nanni Moretti's autobiographical 1993 film

About

Moretti uses his struggle for inspiration and his health worries as the foundation of this film, which wanders around contemporary Italy and explores the Roman suburbs.

Synopsis

There is no plot in the traditional sense. Rather, the film is divided into three chapters: On my Vespa, Islands, and Doctors. The Vespa sequence is a nod to the iconic 1953 Hollywood film Roman Holiday.
